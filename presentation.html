Five layers. One governance seam. Built on AdiPlan, not over it.

Architecture

Five layers, stacked top-down.

Each layer consumes the one below and serves the one above. The seam between Layer 1 and Layer 2 is where AI governance is enforced.

AI Governance

The seam is not a feature. It is the architecture.

Internal proprietary data never reaches an external model. Public and already-published content does. This is the rule that unblocked the APAC pilot.

Internal · Copilot only

  • Adisseo brochures and decks
  • Sales presentations
  • Trial & performance data
  • Customer account records
  • Internal financial reporting
Routing Microsoft Copilot, tenant-bound. Never sent to external LLMs.

Public · External LLMs allowed

  • Scraped competitor news
  • Public scientific papers
  • Adisseo public-website content
  • Industry magazines & reports
  • Raw-material price feeds
Routing External LLMs (GPT, Claude) and the AdiPlan AI synthesis layer.

End-to-end

From scraped article to published deliverable in one flow.

Engagement metrics close the loop. The Malaysia ASF case (7 serious viewers, 3 conversions) becomes the measurement template.

1
Detect
Scraper surfaces a competitor article tagged by species & country.
2
Match
Synthesis layer maps article to a CBI, persona, and target market.
3
Compose
Content studio drafts 3 formats — leaflet, emailer, video script — in the species manager's voice.
4
Audit
Brand-guardrail audit log written. Manager reviews and approves before publishing.
5
Publish
Channel routing: LinkedIn · WeChat · email · magazine · sales handout. Engagement tracked back to step 1.
7 → 3
Malaysia ASF benchmark
7 serious viewers (>2.5 min watch time) · 3 customer conversions. Institutionalized as the engagement metric for all future campaigns.
